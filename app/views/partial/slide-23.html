• Naming convention - don't use prefix $ and $$
    $ for Angular built-in services
    $$ for Angular private

• Minification - solution for DI
    Incorrect
angular.module('angularOnCoffeeApp')
  .controller 'MainCtrl', ($scope)->
    ...
    Correct
angular.module('angularOnCoffeeApp')
  .controller 'MainCtrl', ['$scope', ($scope)->
    ...
  ]
